Democratic Renewal

  • Establish a Citizens' Chamber (200 randomly selected Nepalis, stratified by diversity) to review major laws, constitutional amendments, and set deliberative agendas.
  • National initiative & referendum with a double majority (people + provinces), but only after public deliberation.
  • Open-list proportional representation so voters choose people, not just party ranks.
  • Campaign finance sunlight: Real-time dashboards of all donations and spending.
  • Pilot Quadratic Voting and liquid democracy tools inside party structures and participatory budgeting.
03

Clean Governance

  • Transform the CIAA into a truly independent body with citizen oversight, non-renewable terms, and open contracting by default.
  • All tenders, contracts, and government budgets must be published in machine-readable formats.
  • Digital audit logs: every time an official accesses your record, you can see it. Misuse = automatic penalties.
04

Social Freedoms & Rights

Free Speech & Protest

  • Abolish criminal defamation and "blasphemy/insulting religion" laws.
  • Only true incitement to violence/discrimination (Rabat threshold) is criminal.
  • End blanket bans on platforms‚Äîonly judge-ordered, URL-specific takedowns, published publicly.

Religion & Secularism

  • Amend "secularism" definition to mean strict state neutrality‚Äîno faith gets privilege.
  • Decriminalize voluntary conversion; criminalize only coercion/fraud.
  • Religious trusts must publish audited accounts.

Gender & LGBTQIA+ Equality

  • Enshrine marriage equality constitutionally: gender-neutral civil marriage, equal adoption, inheritance, pensions.
  • Protect self-determined gender identity; ban non-consensual intersex surgeries.
  • Enforce anti-discrimination in work, housing, and education.

Caste & Inclusion

  • Keep reservation (affirmative action) but make it dynamic: quotas recalculated every 5 years based on a Disadvantage Index.
  • Mandatory diversity reporting in public contracts and hiring.
  • Supercharge enforcement with fast-track courts and victim funds.

Startup-Driven Economy

Nepal's Digital Revolution

  • Startup Nepal Mission: 10,000 startups by 2030, 100,000 new jobs created, and $1 billion in export revenue.
  • Government Startup Fund: $50 million venture fund per province, $200 million national fund through public-private partnerships.
  • Startup Universities: Startup academies in every province, international-level incubation and acceleration programs.
  • Digital Citizenship: Special visas for startup founders, remote work permits, and digital nomad facilities.

Green Technology & Innovation

  • Green Startup Hub: Innovation in hydropower technology, solar energy, wind energy, and green transportation.
  • AgriTech Revolution: Support for smart agriculture, precision farming, and agricultural technology startups.
  • Climate Tech: Climate change solutions, carbon capture, and environmental monitoring technology.

Digital Economy & Services

  • FinTech Nepal: Digital banking, mobile payments, and financial inclusion startups.
  • EdTech & HealthTech: Remote education, telemedicine, and digital health service innovations.
  • Tourism Tech: Eco-tourism, cultural tourism, and smart tourism solutions.

Startup Support System

  • One-Window Service: Startup registration, licensing, and tax facilitation within 24 hours.
  • Startup Tax Holiday: 100% tax exemption for first 5 years, 50% tax exemption for next 5 years.
  • International Partnerships: Startup exchange programs with Silicon Valley, Bangalore, and Singapore.
  • Women & Youth Entrepreneurship: Special funds, mentorship programs, and gender equality incentives.

Traditional Economy Modernization

  • Green economy drive: Hydropower value-chains, eco-tourism, agro-processing, and IT exports.
  • Participatory budgeting: 10% of all municipal budgets allocated directly by citizens each year.
  • Universal digital infrastructure: free e-ID, internet access zones, digital literacy by grade 6.
